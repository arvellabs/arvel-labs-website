Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Arvel Labs collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Last updated: April 22, 2026

01Who we are

Arvel Labs ("we", "us", "our") is a technology lab based in Marina del Rey, California. We can be reached at [email protected].

02Information we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to run our business and respond to inquiries:

  • Contact form submissions: first name, last name, email address, area of interest, and any message you send us.
  • Basic analytics: non-identifying usage information such as pages viewed, referrer, browser type, and approximate location — used to understand site performance.
  • Correspondence: any emails, calls, or messages you send us directly.

03How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss potential projects.
  • To send you requested information about our services or products.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

04How we store your information

Contact form submissions are delivered to our business email inbox via a secure form-relay service. We retain inquiries for as long as reasonably necessary to serve the purpose they were collected for, typically up to 24 months, unless a longer period is required by law.

05Third-party services

We rely on a small set of third-party providers to run the website and our business — including form delivery (FormSubmit), hosting, email, and analytics. These providers handle data strictly on our behalf and under their own privacy terms.

06Cookies

Our website may use a small number of cookies for essential functionality and aggregate analytics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; this will not prevent you from browsing the site.

07Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, email [email protected] and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

08Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your data with care and only retain what we need.

09Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

10Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be flagged on the site.
